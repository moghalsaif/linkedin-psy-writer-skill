# linkedin-psychology-writer

A Claude skill for writing LinkedIn posts that trigger emotional responses — not just deliver information.

## Why I built this

I kept noticing a pattern: founders with genuinely useful things to say were getting ignored on LinkedIn, while people with less expertise were booking calls every week. The difference wasn't the information. It was how the content made people *feel*.

I built this skill to encode that gap into something repeatable. Instead of writing posts from scratch and hoping they land, this gives Claude a structured framework to pick the right emotional trigger, write a hook that stops the scroll, and avoid the AI-writing patterns that sophisticated audiences spot immediately.

## What it does

- Identifies the right post type: standard engagement, lead magnet, or TOF belief disruption
- Selects a primary + secondary emotional trigger before writing a single word
- Follows hook formulas tailored to each trigger type
- Stacks FOMO layers for lead magnet posts
- Runs an authenticity filter before finalizing
- Outputs two versions: conservative (close to your voice) and aggressive (optimized for reach)
- Hardcodes a list of banned AI-writing patterns so posts don't read as generated
